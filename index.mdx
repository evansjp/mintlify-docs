## Welcome to Turret

Turret is a product analytics API that helps AI app developers understand how users interact with their applications. By semantically clustering free text metadata from user events, Turret detects patterns in user behavior that traditional analytics miss.

Perfect for Product Managers who want to understand how people are prompting their LLMs and discover insights about user intent.
